# Tokenist

## Product purpose
Personal-use iOS + macOS app that shows Claude.ai subscription usage at a glance — 5-hour session %, weekly all-models %, Fable / Opus / Sonnet weekly sub-caps, and extra spending. Built for developers heavy on Claude Code / claude.ai who want to know when they're about to hit limits without opening the website.

## Register
product

## Users
One developer (the author), plus technically savvy friends willing to clone, configure signing, and paste a session cookie. **Not** general consumers, **not** App Store shoppers, **not** non-technical users. Distribution is GitHub-only.

## Brand and tone
- Practical, low-ceremony, native-feeling
- Quiet over loud. Should feel like a system utility, not a SaaS product
- Confident about being a hack: README leads with "personal use only, unofficial endpoint"
- Numbers and reset countdowns are the content; everything else is chrome

## Anti-references
- Not Anthropic's official Claude.ai chrome (no Anthropic orange, no Claude logo)
- Not enterprise SaaS dashboard heaviness, no big-number hero cliché
- Not consumer wellness app playfulness or illustrations
- Not glassmorphism-as-decoration: if Liquid Glass materials are used, they must serve readability — never just to look fancy

## Strategic principles
- Feel native to iOS 26 / macOS 26 — use system materials and patterns, don't fight the platform
- One color identity used sparingly: a calm blue derived from the app icon
- Cookie-paste onboarding is necessary friction, but should be calm and matter-of-fact, not apologetic or hand-wavy
- Liquid Glass is appropriate here because the app's content is small (a few numbers) and the surface can become atmospheric without crowding information
